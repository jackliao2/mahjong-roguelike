Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

The short version: We do not use cookies. No tracking cookies, no advertising cookies, no session cookies. You will never see a cookie consent banner on our site because we don't need one. We use browser localStorage for game progress (which is not a cookie) and Umami for cookie-free analytics.

1. Our Cookie-Free Approach

Unlike most websites, Mahjong Roguelike does not use any cookies. We have designed the Game from the ground up to respect your privacy and comply with cookie regulations without requiring intrusive consent banners.

Under the EU ePrivacy Directive (the "Cookie Law"), websites must obtain user consent before placing non-essential cookies. Because we do not use any cookies, this requirement does not apply to our site.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to track users across sessions and sites, enable advertising networks, and store authentication state. Common types include:

  • Tracking cookies — Follow your activity across websites (we don't use these).
  • Advertising cookies — Enable targeted ads (we don't use these).
  • Session cookies — Maintain login state (we don't use these; no accounts).
  • Preference cookies — Remember settings (we use localStorage instead).

4. LocalStorage Details

The Game uses the browser's localStorage API to save your progress. localStorage is fundamentally different from cookies:

  • Not transmitted: localStorage data never leaves your browser. Cookies are sent with every HTTP request.
  • Larger capacity: localStorage can store up to 5-10MB; cookies are limited to 4KB.
  • No expiration: localStorage persists until you clear it; cookies have expiration dates.
  • Not tracked: localStorage cannot be read by other websites; cookies can be read by third-party domains.

The following localStorage keys are used:

  • mjrg_run — Current run state (round, score, relics, custom tiles)
  • mjrg_meta — Meta-progression (total runs, wins, best score, achievements)
  • mjrg_yaku_bonuses — Active yaku boost bonuses

You can clear these at any time via your browser's developer tools (Application → Local Storage) or browser settings (Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data).

6. Third-Party Cookies

While we do not set any cookies ourselves, some third-party services may set cookies when you visit our site:

  • Google Fonts — Google's font CDN may set cookies. These are controlled by Google, not us. See Google's cookie policy. You can block these with browser extensions without affecting gameplay.
  • Hosting provider — Our hosting platform (Vercel or Cloudflare) may set essential operational cookies for security (e.g., DDoS protection). These are typically "__cf_" prefix cookies and are necessary for the site to function.

We do not control and are not responsible for third-party cookies. To block all third-party cookies, enable "Block third-party cookies" in your browser settings.

7. How to Manage Your Data

Since we don't use cookies, managing your data is straightforward:

  • To clear game progress: Open your browser settings → Clear browsing data → select "Cookies and site data" → clear. Or use the "Reset Progress" button in the Game.
  • To block analytics: Install an ad blocker (uBlock Origin, AdGuard) or add the Umami domain to your blocklist. This will not affect gameplay.
  • To block Google Fonts: Use a browser extension or disable web fonts in your browser settings. The Game will fall back to system fonts.
  • To browse in private: Use Incognito/Private Browsing mode. Your game progress will not be saved when you close the window.

8. Regulatory Compliance

Our cookie-free approach ensures compliance with:

  • EU ePrivacy Directive — No non-essential cookies means no consent banner is required.
  • GDPR — No personal data is processed through cookies. See our Privacy Policy.
  • CCPA — No cookies are used to sell or share personal data.
  • UK PECR — Compliant with UK Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations.
  • COPPA — No tracking of children through cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever introduce cookies (for example, for a future premium feature), we will:

  • Update this Cookie Policy with details of each cookie used.
  • Implement a consent management platform (CMP) in compliance with GDPR.
  • Provide granular opt-in/opt-out controls.
  • Only set non-essential cookies after obtaining explicit consent.

Until then, you can enjoy the Game knowing no cookies are tracking you.
