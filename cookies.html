Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

The short version: The Game uses localStorage to save progress. We do not set our own account or session cookies. If advertising is enabled on content pages, Google or another advertising provider may use cookies or similar technologies as described below.

1. Our Storage Approach

Mahjong Quiz is designed to work without accounts. The Game saves progress in browser localStorage, not in an account database. We do not set our own login, account, or session cookies.

Some third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies, especially if display advertising is enabled on article, guide, or blog pages. Where required by law, non-essential cookies are controlled through a consent flow.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to track users across sessions and sites, enable advertising networks, and store authentication state. Common types include:

  • Tracking cookies - Follow activity across websites.
  • Advertising cookies - Enable ad serving, measurement, fraud prevention, frequency capping, and sometimes personalization.
  • Session cookies — Maintain login state (we don't use these; no accounts).
  • Preference cookies — Remember settings (we use localStorage instead).

4. LocalStorage Details

The Game uses the browser's localStorage API to save your progress. localStorage is fundamentally different from cookies:

  • Not transmitted: localStorage data never leaves your browser. Cookies are sent with every HTTP request.
  • Larger capacity: localStorage can store up to 5-10MB; cookies are limited to 4KB.
  • No expiration: localStorage persists until you clear it; cookies have expiration dates.
  • Not tracked: localStorage cannot be read by other websites; cookies can be read by third-party domains.

The following localStorage keys are used:

  • mjrg_run — Current run state (round, score, relics, custom tiles)
  • mjrg_meta — Meta-progression (total runs, wins, best score, achievements)
  • mjrg_yaku_bonuses — Active yaku boost bonuses

The Game may also use sessionStorage for ephemeral UI state (such as which tooltip is currently shown or which scene is in transition). sessionStorage behaves like localStorage but is automatically cleared when you close the browser tab. It is never transmitted to any server and is not a cookie.

As a Progressive Web App (PWA), the Game registers a service worker that caches static assets (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images) in a Cache API storage. This enables offline play after the first visit. This cache is also browser-local, not transmitted, and not a cookie. You can clear it via your browser's "Site data" settings.

You can clear all of these at any time via your browser's developer tools (Application → Local Storage / Session Storage / Cache Storage) or browser settings (Clear browsing data → Cookies and site data).

6. Third-Party Cookies

While we do not set our own account or session cookies, some third-party services may set cookies or use similar technologies when you visit our site:

  • Google Fonts - Google's font CDN may set cookies. These are controlled by Google, not us. See Google's cookie policy. You can block these with browser extensions without affecting gameplay.
  • Google AdSense / Google advertising services - If enabled, Google may use cookies or similar technologies to serve and measure ads, prevent fraud and abuse, limit ad frequency, and personalize ads where legally permitted. See Google's advertising technologies policy.
  • Hosting provider - Our hosting platform may set essential operational cookies for security and abuse prevention.

We do not control and are not responsible for third-party cookies. To block all third-party cookies, enable "Block third-party cookies" in your browser settings.

7. Server Logs (Hosting Provider)

While we do not set cookies, our hosting provider (Vercel or Cloudflare Pages) automatically records standard server request logs when you load the Game. These logs typically include:

  • IP address (used for security/abuse detection, not linked to identity)
  • Timestamp of the request
  • URL requested and HTTP method
  • User-Agent string (browser and operating system)
  • Referrer URL
  • Response status code

These logs are not cookies — they are server-side records controlled by the hosting provider, not by your browser. They are retained for up to 30 days for security and operational purposes, then automatically deleted. We do not have direct access to these logs for analytics; they are used solely for infrastructure protection and abuse mitigation.

For details on how your hosting provider handles logs, see Vercel's Privacy Policy or Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

8. How to Manage Your Data

You can manage site data through your browser. This includes localStorage, sessionStorage, service worker cache, and any third-party cookies:

8.1 Browser-Specific Instructions

  • Google Chrome — Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data → select "Cookies and other site data" and "Cached images and files."
  • Mozilla Firefox — Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data → Clear Data.
  • Apple Safari — Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data → Remove All.
  • Microsoft Edge — Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies and site data.
  • Brave — Settings → Additional Settings → Privacy and security → Clear browsing data.

8.2 Quick Actions

  • To clear game progress: Use the "Reset Progress" button in the Game's settings menu, or clear site data via your browser as described above.
  • To block analytics: Install an ad blocker (uBlock Origin, AdGuard) or add the Umami domain to your blocklist. This will not affect gameplay.
  • To block Google Fonts: Use a browser extension (e.g., uBlock Origin's "Block remote fonts") or disable web fonts in your browser settings. The Game will fall back to system fonts.
  • To disable service worker / PWA: Use your browser's developer tools (Application → Service Workers → Unregister). The Game will still work but will not be available offline.
  • To browse privately: Use Incognito/Private Browsing mode. Your game progress will not be saved when you close the window.
