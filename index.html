Not mahjong solitaire. This is real Japanese riichi mahjong — the 4-player tile game from anime like Akagi and Saki, not the matching-pairs solitaire you find on most "mahjong" websites. Looking to learn the real thing? You're in the right place.

Build Hands

Draw, discard, and call tiles to assemble a 14-tile hand. Form sequences, triplets, and pairs across runs of man, pin, and sou suits.

Score Yaku

Complete winning patterns called yaku. Each yaku earns han and multiplies your score. Stack them for explosive combo finishes.

Collect Relics

After each win, draft powerful relics that bend the rules. Reroll tiles, double your yaku, or break the fundamental laws of mahjong.
