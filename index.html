Read the Hand

Each question shows a mahjong hand and a clear prompt. Is it asking for the winning tile, the right discard, the yaku builder, or the waiting tiles?

Pick the Answer

Four tile options are shown. Tap the tile that correctly answers the prompt. Get it right to keep your run alive; get it wrong and the run ends.

Climb the Chapters

Every three questions form a chapter. The last question is a BOSS with bonus points. Clear it to draft a relic, choose a path, and push deeper.
