1. Overview

Mahjong Roguelike ("we", "us", or "the Game") is a free browser-based game operated by the Mahjong Roguelike team. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you visit our website at mahjongroguelike.com and play the Game.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Game is designed to be playable without any account, email address, or personally identifiable information. We comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate and improve the Game:

2.1 Data You Provide

  • None. We do not require accounts, email addresses, names, or any registration. The Game is playable anonymously.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Anonymous analytics data via Umami (see Section 4 for details). This includes page views, referrer URL, country (derived from IP, not stored), browser type, and device type — all in aggregate, never linked to individuals.
  • Game progress data stored locally in your browser (see Section 3). This never leaves your device.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, phone number, or physical address
  • Your exact IP address (Umami derives country-level location only, then discards the IP)
  • Your browsing history on other websites
  • Personal identifiers or tracking IDs across sessions
  • Biometric or health data
  • Payment information (the Game is free; no payment processing occurs)

3. Local Storage & Game Data

The Game stores your progress — including your current run state, unlocked achievements, high scores, and settings — in your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Exists only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • Is tied to the specific browser and device you are using.
  • Will be lost if you clear your browser data or use a different browser/device.
  • Contains no personal information — only game state (e.g., round number, score, relics collected).

The specific localStorage keys we use are:

  • mjrg_run — Current run state (round, score, relics, custom tiles)
  • mjrg_meta — Meta-progression (total runs, wins, best score, achievements)
  • mjrg_yaku_bonuses — Active yaku boost bonuses for the current run

You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings or by clicking "Reset Progress" in the Game.

4. Analytics (Umami)

We use Umami, an open-source, privacy-focused web analytics platform. Unlike Google Analytics, Umami:

  • Does not use cookies — no tracking cookies of any kind.
  • Does not collect or store IP addresses — country-level location is derived in-memory and immediately discarded.
  • Does not track individuals — all data is aggregated and anonymous.
  • Does not fingerprint devices — no canvas fingerprinting, no cross-site tracking.
  • Is GDPR compliant by design — no consent banner required because no personal data is processed.

The analytics data we receive includes: total page views, unique visitors (anonymized), referrer URLs, browser type, operating system, and country. This helps us understand which features are popular and improve the Game.

5. Cookies

We do not use cookies. The Game does not set any tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or session cookies. Umami analytics operates without cookies. Your browser's localStorage is used for game progress only, which is not a cookie and is not transmitted to any server.

Because we do not use cookies for tracking, we are not subject to the EU ePrivacy Directive's cookie consent requirements. No cookie banner is displayed because none is needed.

6. Third-Party Services

The Game uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts — We load web fonts (Press Start 2P, Inter) from Google's CDN. Google may log the request including your IP address. See Google's Privacy Policy. This is a standard web font service; no personal data is shared by us.
  • Umami Analytics — As described in Section 4. Privacy-friendly, no cookies, no personal data.
  • Hosting provider — The Game is hosted on a static hosting platform (Vercel or Cloudflare Pages). The hosting provider may log standard server request data (IP, timestamp, URL) for security and operational purposes.

We do not sell, rent, or share any data with advertising networks, social media platforms, or data brokers.

7. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have the following rights under the GDPR:

  • Right of Access — You can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you. Since we do not collect personal data, this will typically return an empty result.
  • Right to Rectification — You can request correction of inaccurate data. Since we do not collect personal data, this is not applicable.
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten") — You can request deletion of your data. Since all game data is stored locally in your browser, you can erase it by clearing your browser's localStorage or site data.
  • Right to Restrict Processing — You can request that we limit how we use your data.
  • Right to Data Portability — You can request your data in a machine-readable format. Your local game data can be exported from your browser's developer tools.
  • Right to Object — You can object to our processing of your data.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent — Since we do not process personal data based on consent (Umami does not require consent), this is not applicable. However, you can block analytics by using an ad blocker or disabling JavaScript for the analytics domain.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@mahjongroguelike.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Your Rights Under CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to Know — You can request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal data we collect. Since we do not collect personal data as defined by CCPA, this will typically return "no data collected."
  • Right to Delete — You can request deletion of your personal data. Your local game data can be cleared from your browser settings.
  • Right to Opt-Out — You can opt out of the "sale" of your personal data. We do not sell personal data, so this right is not applicable. However, you can opt out of anonymous analytics by blocking the Umami script.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@mahjongroguelike.com.

9. Children's Privacy

The Game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal data from children or adults. We do not direct marketing at children, and no account creation is required. Since we do not collect personal data, the Game is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the GDPR's provisions regarding children's data.

If you believe a child has provided personal data (which should not be possible given our data collection practices), please contact us immediately and we will take appropriate action.

10. Changes to This Policy

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Provide a summary of material changes in the Game's changelog.
  • Notify users of significant changes through an in-game message.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
