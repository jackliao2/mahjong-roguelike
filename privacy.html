1. Overview

Mahjong Quiz ("we", "us", or "the Game") is a free browser-based game operated by the Mahjong Quiz team. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you visit our website at mahjongroguelike.com and play the Game.

Mahjong Quiz is designed to be playable without an account. This policy describes the limited information involved in serving the site, saving progress locally, and responding when you choose to contact us. References to regional privacy rights apply where the relevant law applies to the site operator.

2. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to operate and improve the Game:

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Contact messages. If you use the Contact page, the page prepares an email containing the name, reply address, topic, and message you entered. Your email app sends it only if you choose to complete sending.
  • No registration data. Playing the Game does not require an account, name, or email address.

2.2 Data Collected Automatically

  • Hosting request data. Vercel may process IP address, request URL, timestamp, user agent, and security-related request information when serving the site.
  • Font requests. Content pages currently request fonts from Google Fonts, so Google receives the technical information ordinarily included in that request.
  • Game progress data stored locally in your browser (see Section 4). The current application does not send these values to us.

2.3 Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your phone number or physical address through the Game
  • Your browsing history on other websites
  • Biometric or health data
  • Payment information (the Game is free; no payment processing occurs)

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

Where the GDPR applies, the relevant legal bases for the limited processing described here are:

  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)) — Operating, securing, and troubleshooting the website, and responding to messages you choose to send.
  • Steps requested by you (Art. 6(1)(b)) — Responding to licensing inquiries where you ask us to consider a potential agreement.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) — If a future optional service requires consent, it will not be enabled for that purpose until an appropriate choice is offered.

The Game does not ask you to provide special-category data. Please do not include sensitive personal information in a contact message.

4. Local Storage & Game Data

The Game stores your progress — including your current run state, unlocked achievements, high scores, and settings — in your browser's localStorage. This data:

  • Exists in your browser; the current application code does not transmit it to us.
  • Is tied to the specific browser and device you are using.
  • Will be lost if you clear your browser data or use a different browser/device.
  • Contains game state and preferences rather than account profile data.

The specific localStorage keys we use are:

  • mjrg_run — Current run state (round, score, relics, custom tiles)
  • mjrg_meta — Meta-progression (total runs, wins, best score, achievements)
  • mjrg_settings, mjrg_sound_muted — Gameplay and sound preferences
  • mjrg_yaku_bonuses — Active yaku boost bonuses for the current run
  • mjrg_leaderboard, mjrg_daily, mjrg_mistakes — Local scores, daily-challenge state, and review history
  • mjrg_onboarded, mjrg_beginner_done, mjrg_tutorial_seen, mjrg_normal_done — Tutorial and mode-completion state
  • mjrg_cookies_accepted — Acknowledgement that the storage notice was dismissed

You can clear this data at any time through your browser's site-data settings.

5. Analytics

No analytics provider is currently loaded on the public site. An inactive code integration point sends nothing without a separately configured analytics script. If analytics is enabled later, this policy will be updated before or when that change takes effect.

6. Advertising

No advertising provider is currently loaded on the public site. Some pages contain empty layout placeholders that may be used for ads in the future. Before enabling an advertising provider, we will update this policy and add consent or opt-out controls where required.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

The Game uses browser localStorage for game progress. Browsers do not automatically attach localStorage values to HTTP requests, and the current application code does not transmit those values. Same-origin JavaScript can technically read localStorage, so this policy will be updated if future code begins sending any of it.

We do not currently set account, login, or advertising cookies. The first-visit privacy notice stores only a local acknowledgement key. See the Cookie and Local Storage Policy for the current storage inventory.

8. Third-Party Services

The current public site uses:

  • Vercel — Hosts and delivers the static site and may process ordinary request and security logs. See Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — Delivers fonts on content pages and receives the technical information included in font requests. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Your email provider — Processes a contact message only if you complete sending the prepared email in your own mail app.

We do not sell personal information and do not currently use cross-context behavioral advertising.

9. International Data Transfers

Vercel and Google operate global infrastructure, so request data may be processed outside your country. Their privacy notices describe the safeguards they use. Local game progress remains on your device unless future functionality is introduced and disclosed.

10. Data Retention

  • Local game progress — Remains in your browser until you clear this site's data.
  • Contact email — May be retained in the receiving mailbox while needed to answer the inquiry, maintain business records, resolve disputes, or meet legal obligations.
  • Hosting and font request data — Is retained under the applicable provider's own settings and policies; we do not promise a provider retention period that we do not control.

11. Security Measures

We use reasonable measures appropriate to the limited information involved:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption — All traffic between your browser and our servers is encrypted in transit.
  • Static architecture — The current Game does not use an account system or application database.
  • No stored credentials — The current Game has no password database.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to security@mahjongroguelike.com.

12. Your Rights Under GDPR

If applicable law grants you privacy rights, those may include access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, objection, withdrawal of consent, and the right to complain to a supervisory authority. These rights can be subject to legal conditions and exceptions.

  • Local game progress can be deleted directly through your browser's site-data controls; because the current application does not send it to us, we cannot remotely identify or delete it.
  • For contact email or other information we control, send a request to the privacy address below. We may need enough information to verify and locate the relevant correspondence.

To exercise a right, contact privacy@mahjongroguelike.com. We will respond within the period required by applicable law.

13. Your Rights Under CCPA

If the California Consumer Privacy Act applies to the site operator and your information, you may have rights to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale or sharing, limit certain uses of sensitive information, and receive non-discriminatory treatment. We do not currently sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Requests may be sent to privacy@mahjongroguelike.com.

14. Children's Privacy

The service is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically to children under 13. Children under 13 should not submit their name, email address, or other personal information through the Contact page. If you believe a child has sent personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the Game or its data practices change. We will update the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, provide a prominent notice before or when a material change takes effect. Please review this page periodically.

16. Contact Us
